About Me

Hello! I am a Technical Designer and Software Engineer. I was unsure of my specialization when I entered school, but now with 4 years of game projects and 2 Steam releases, I have found my niche acting as a dedicated programmer for my artists and designers.

I love working with my team to understand the problem points, and implementing systems that are easy for them to use and easy for me to update as needs change. As a technical designer, I have the skills to be flexible and proactive to my teams' specific needs in my approach to problem-solving.
